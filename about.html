More About Me

A picture of me

Hello once again, and if it wasn't obvious my name is Mitchell Carney! I have been working with computers since at least middle school, although, really it started much before that. In elementary school I was able to mess around a little with Scratch, which is a simple drag-and-drop coding site. My interest in computers grew throughout the remainder of my K-12 years. In fact, some of the projects you see below I completed in high school, when I learned Python for the first time (My first real experience with coding). In 2020 I started at Saint Petersburg Collegiate High School where I studied Dual Enrolled at Saint Petersburg College for an AA in Computer Science. I was able to learn some C and C++ there and graduated in 2023. In fall of 2023 I started at USF, where I currently am working on my Bachelors in Computer Engineering (Set to graduate May 2026). I switched to the Computer Engineering route becuase I felt it was a more versatile major, and I do have an interest in both Hardware and Software (but primarily software). At USF I have continued to learn more C and Python, while recently, in summer 2025 I took the Global Career Accelerator where I learned to work with Python and SQL to analyze databases, while also studying HTML, CSS and JavaScript independently.
